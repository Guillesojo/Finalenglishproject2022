Future Progressive Tense


We use future progressive to describe an action that will be in progress at a future time.

Formula (positive): Subject + will + be + verb + ing + complement.
Formula (negative): Subject + will + not + be + verb + ing + complement
Formula (question): Will + subject + be + verb + ing + complement + ?
