Simple Future Tense


We use simple future to talk about things and actions that haven’t happened yet.

Formula (positive): Subject + will/shall + verb in base form + complement
Formula (negative): Subject + will + not + verb in base form + complement
Formula (question): Will + subject + verb in base form + complement + ?
