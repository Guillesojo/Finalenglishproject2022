Simple Present Tense


We use simple present to describe habits, facts, actions happening now, permanent actions and plans.

Formula (positive): Subject + verb in base form + complement.
Formula (negative): Subject + don't ( I, you, we, they,) or doesnÂ´t ( He, She, It) + verb in base form + complement.
Formula (question): Do ( I, you, we, they) or Does ( He, She, It) + subject + verb in base form + complement + ?

Note that in case your subject is he, she or it, you need to include s, es or ies at the end of your verb. Which one to use depends on each verb.
