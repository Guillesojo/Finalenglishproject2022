Simple Past Tense


We use simple past to talk about completed actions and things that existed before now.

Formula (positive): Subject + verb + d, ed or modifications + complement.
Formula (negative): Subject + didn´t + unchanged verb + complement.
Formula (question): Did + subject + unchanged verb + complement?

Note that we terminate the verbs with ed or d. In other cases, we change the structure of the verb completely (modifications). You can see are the irregular the verbs list here.
