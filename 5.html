Present Progressive Tense


We use present progressive to describe an action that is in progress right now.

Formula (positive): Subject + verb to be (am , is, are) + verb + ing + complement.
Formula (negative): Subject + verb to be (am , is, are) + not + verb in base form + ing + complement.
Formula (question): Verb to be (am , is, are) + subject + verb + ing + complement + ?
