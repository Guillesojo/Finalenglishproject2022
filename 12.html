Passive Voice


We use passive voice when we want to emphasize the action and the object of a sentence rather than subject.

When to use it:
- Reports of crimes or incidents with unknown perpetrators.
- Scientific contexts
- When you want to emphasize the actions and the doer of the actions is irrelevant or distracting.

When not to use it:
- Academic writing
- When you want to be dirext
