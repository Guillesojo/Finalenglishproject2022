Past Progressive Tense


We use past progressive to describe an action that was in progress at a specific time in the past.

Formula (positive): Subject + were (you, we and they) / was (He, she, it, I) + verb + ing + complement negative
Formula (negative): Subject + were (you, we and they) / was (I, He, she, it) + not + verb + ing + complement
Formula (question): Were (you, we, and they)/ was (I, He, she, it) + subject + verb + ing + complement + ?
